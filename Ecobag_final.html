Who We Are

Sustainable Packaging
Born in Addis Ababa

EcoBag Addis is a small-scale e-commerce business dedicated to providing environmentally friendly paper bags as a sustainable alternative to plastic packaging. We were founded in response to growing environmental concerns about plastic waste — which pollutes streets, rivers, and drainage systems across Ethiopia.

Our online platform lets customers browse, compare, and order high-quality paper bags with full customization options — from logos to sizes — without ever leaving their home or office.

⚖️
Backed by Law

Under Ethiopia's Solid Waste Management Proclamation 1383/2017, the production, import, sale, and use of single-use plastic bags is now prohibited. EcoBag Addis is your compliant, convenient alternative.
